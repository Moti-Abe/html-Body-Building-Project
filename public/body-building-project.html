Best 5 YouTube Channels for Muscle Building

  1. ATHLEAN-X YouTube Channel

    • Focus: Science-based training, injury prevention, muscle building.
    • Why it’s great: Jeff is a physical therapist and strength coach.
      He explains how muscles work, proper form, and how to train smart.

  2. Jeremy Ethier YouTube Channel

    • Focus: Evidence-based workout programs and tips.
    • Why it’s great: He breaks down complex topics using studies and
      visuals, with practical workouts that are easy to follow.

  3. Jeff Nippard YouTube Channel

    • Focus: Science and bodybuilding.
    • Why it’s great: Jeff combines research with personal experience,
      ideal for those who want to learn the “why” behind exercises.

  4. Fraser Wilson YouTube Channel

    • Focus: Intense home & gym workouts, fitness motivation.
    • Why it’s great: Known for quick & intense routines,
      he offers great home workouts with minimal equipment.

  5. Sofonias Ethiopian YouTube Channel

    • Focus: Science-based training, local foods for bodybuilding.
    • Why it’s great: It’s best for Ethiopian youth who don’t understand foreign languages,
      but want to learn the science behind bodybuilding.
